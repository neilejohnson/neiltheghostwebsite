E + H is a simple love story wrapped in an experiment. I set out to make a comic in which we would follow one character's panels horizontally (left to right) and the other's vertically (top to bottom). In order to keep it coherent, I found it important to make the plot as simple as possible and to include no dialogue. The latter was something I wanted to try my hand at for a while anyway, as most of my other comics can get a bit wordy and I like the idea of someone being able to read one of my comics without knowing English as a prerequisite.
